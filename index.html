Crystal Collector

How to Play!

  1. 1. When you start the game, you will be given a random target number. Your goal is to match your score to the target number.
  2. 2. Check out the crystals below. When you click on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your score.
  3. 3. When you reach the target number, you win! But if you pass the target number, you lose!
  4. 4. But wait! The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time you start a new game, the value of each crystal will change.

Good luck!
